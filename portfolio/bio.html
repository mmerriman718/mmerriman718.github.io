About the Artist

Milo Merriman is a digital and traditional media artist from San Jose, CA. He is also a writer, a musician, and an animator. He loves to create stories that depict human interaction and emotion. He enjoys creating whimsical and dramatic scenes full of light, color, and feeling. He draws inspiration from his writing, as well as other artists he admires. With his passion for characters and storytelling, Milo hopes to use his talents to assist media studios in bringing their own stories to life.
